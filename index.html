FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the Ofast Toolkit slow down my website?

Absolutely not. The Ofast Toolkit is built on a strictly modular architecture. If you don't need a specific feature (like Forms), simply toggle it off. Disabled modules load zero assets and execute zero background code, ensuring your site stays lightning-fast.

Do I need to activate all the modules?

No. Every module in Ofast Toolkit can be independently enabled or disabled from the Toolkit dashboard. Only activate what you need; inactive modules add zero overhead to your site.

Is this compatible with my theme?

Yes. Ofast Toolkit operates entirely within the WordPress admin and backend. It does not interfere with your theme or frontend design.

What happens if my primary SMTP server goes down?

If you are an Ofast Pro user, our intelligent Fallback Host feature automatically detects the failure and instantly routes the email through your configured secondary provider, ensuring uninterrupted delivery.

How does the licensing work?

Upon purchase, your license key is automatically generated and securely stored in your Ofast account. You can easily view it in your "My Downloads" dashboard. Enter this key into the plugin settings to unlock Pro features and enable seamless automatic updates.

Do you offer support?

Yes. Free users can access community support. Pro users receive priority email support. Reach us at support@ofastshop.com
